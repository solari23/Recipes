# Pumpkin Spice Mix

Makes about 3/4 cup of pumpkin spice mixture.

## Ingredients
* 1/4 cup ground cinnamon
* 2 tbsp ground ginger
* 2 tsp ground nutmeg
* 2 tsp ground cloves
* 1/4 tsp cardamom

## Preparation
1. Mix the spices together until fully mixed.
1. Store in an airtight containter in a cool, dry place.
